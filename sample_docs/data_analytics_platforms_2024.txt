Competitive Intelligence Report: Data Analytics Platforms 2024
Report Date: 2024-04-15
Effective Date: 2024-04-15
Prepared by: Market Intelligence Division

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The data analytics and business intelligence market reached $35 billion in 2024, with semantic layers, real-time analytics, and AI-augmented insights driving platform evolution. This report covers competitive dynamics among data warehouse, BI, and analytics vendors.

KEY PARTIES AND MARKET POSITIONS

1. Snowflake Inc. - Cloud Data Platform
   Role: Leading cloud data warehouse with data sharing capabilities
   Market Cap: $62 billion
   Revenue: $2.8 billion (36% YoY growth)
   Customers: 9,400+ (including 639 Forbes Global 2000)

2. Databricks Inc. - Unified Analytics Platform
   Role: Data lakehouse pioneer combining warehousing and data science
   Valuation: $43 billion
   ARR: $1.6 billion (60% YoY growth)
   Customers: 10,000+ organizations

3. Tableau (Salesforce) - Business Intelligence
   Role: Market-leading data visualization and self-service BI
   Revenue: $1.9 billion (estimated within Salesforce)
   Users: 1 million+ active creators

4. Microsoft Power BI - Enterprise BI Platform
   Role: Fastest-growing BI tool leveraging Microsoft ecosystem
   Users: 15 million+ monthly active
   Pricing: $10/user/month (Pro), $20/user/month (Premium per user)

5. dbt Labs Inc. - Analytics Engineering
   Role: Leading analytics transformation framework
   Valuation: $4.2 billion
   Community: 50,000+ active dbt users
   Cloud Customers: 3,500+

Data Platform Pricing:
- Snowflake: $2-$4/credit (compute) + $23/TB/month (storage)
- Databricks: $0.07-$0.65/DBU depending on workload
- Google BigQuery: $6.25/TB queried + $0.02/GB/month storage
- Amazon Redshift Serverless: $0.375/RPU-hour
- Microsoft Fabric: $0.36/CU-hour

BI Tool Pricing:
- Tableau Creator: $75/user/month

- Looker (Google): $5,000/month base + per-user fees
- ThoughtSpot: $95/user/month
- Sigma Computing: $25/user/month

STRATEGIC INITIATIVES

1. Data Lakehouse Convergence
   Snowflake launching Iceberg Tables and Unistore for transactional workloads. Databricks Unity Catalog unifying governance. Convergence of warehouse and lake architectures accelerating.
   Timeline: Feature parity expected by end 2024

2. AI/ML Native Analytics
   All platforms embedding LLM-powered natural language querying. Snowflake Cortex, Databricks AI/BI, and Power BI Copilot enabling conversational analytics.
   Timeline: GA across major platforms Q2-Q3 2024

3. Data Marketplace and Sharing
   Snowflake Marketplace (2,000+ listings) and Databricks Marketplace enabling data monetization. Cross-cloud data sharing becoming competitive differentiator.
   Timeline: $5 billion data marketplace opportunity by 2026

MARKET RISKS AND OPPORTUNITIES

Risks:
1. Cloud Cost Overruns - Consumption-based pricing causing budget unpredictability. Severity: High
2. Data Governance Complexity - Multi-platform data sprawl creating compliance challenges. Severity: Medium-High
3. Vendor Lock-in - Proprietary formats and features limiting portability. Severity: Medium

Opportunities:
1. Real-Time Analytics - Streaming analytics market growing to $12 billion. Potential: High
2. Data Mesh Architecture - Decentralized data ownership model gaining enterprise traction. Potential: Medium-High
3. Embedded Analytics - $8 billion market for analytics within SaaS applications. Potential: High
